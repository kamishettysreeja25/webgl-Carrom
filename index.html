Carrom Board 3D Game

Instructions

      Controls
  • move the striker by right & left arrow keys
  • Increase or Decrease the power by f and s keys
  • a & d to increase and decrease the angle of shooting
  • space : Shoot the striker
  • p : Player View
  • t : Tower View
  • w : Coin View
  • s : Decrease the speed
  • f : Increase the speed
      • Scores
  • Your score starts at 100
  • Score reduces by 1 point every 5 seconds and by 20 points for pocketing a coin of the opposite colour or the striker.
  • You get 5 points for pocketing a coin of your colour.
  • If you pocket the red, you get 20 points if you follow it with a coin of your colour in the next attempt. If not, the red goes back at the centre
